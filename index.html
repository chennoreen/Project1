Network Infrastruction in Wellington

City network infrastructure is the resources of an entire network that enables connectivity and communication between users, services, applications and processes. In Wellington, The markings of the network can be found almost everywhere, if you observe carefully.

Markings- example of network

Sometimes you'll see a manhole cover that is surrounded with colorful markings. Whenever a contractor or construction company plans to do street excavation, utility companies will mark out the location of their underground cables so that the contractor knows to watch out for them

Utility locating companies mark the location of underground utilities using brightly orange colored paint.
